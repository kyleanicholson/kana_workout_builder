Welcome to Kana, the ultimate tool for building and tracking your fitness journey. We understand that everyone's fitness goals are unique, which is why we designed our application to be customizable to your needs. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced athlete, our app will help you build your dream strength training program which you can then easily export to Excel and share with your friends, family or clients.

How it Works

Our application is designed to be as simple and intuitive as possible. To get started, simply create a free account and log in. Once you're logged in, you'll be able to create your own workout programs, or use one of our pre-built templates. Once you've created your program, you can add workouts to it, and then add exercises to each workout. You can also edit or delete any of your programs, workouts or exercises at any time.

We welcome your feedback -- If you have any questions, concerns or suggestions, please get in touch via our contact page.

Create a free account and log in to save, create, and share your customized strength training templates!
