At Kana, we believe exercise is medicine and that weight training for muscle and strength development is essential for health and longevity. With the vision of a healthier and fitter world in mind, our app enables coaches, athletes, and other fitness professionals to design and share training programs. If you routinely design strength training programs, this app will help you create, modify and distribute your programs more efficienly.

How it Works

With its minimalist and user friendly interface, Kana allows you to create and store strength training programs for yourself or your clients. You can create a workout program and adding workouts with unique exercises that match your training goals and progression strategy.

We welcome your feedback -- If you have any questions, concerns or suggestions, please get in touch via our contact page.

Create a free account and log in to save, create, and share your customized strength training templates!
